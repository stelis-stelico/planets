PLANETS OF THE SOLAR SYSTEM

Mercury is the smallest Planet in the Solar System and the nearest Sun. Its slightly larger than Earth's Moon. And its the fastest Planet in the Solar System, that is why it takes the shortest time to orbit the Sun.

Venus is the second Planet from the Sun and its the Earth's closest planetary neighbor. It is the hotest Planet in the Solar System.

Earth our home Planet. The only place inhabited by livings things. It has no rings. Our atmosphere protects Earth from incoming meteoroids, most of which break up in our atmosphere before they can strike the surface.

Mars is the 4th planet from the Sun- a dusty, cold desert world with a very thin atmosphere

Jupiter is a giant Planet in the Solar System. The 5th Planet from the Sun

Its the 6th in the Solar System. It has a complex system of icy rings. Its atmosphere is mostly made up of Hydrogen and Helium.

Uranus is the 7th Planet from the Sun. Its rotates at a 90degress angle from the plane of its orbit. Uranus is an ice giant
